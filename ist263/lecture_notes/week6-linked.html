Week 6 Notes

What are wireframes? Create a website's layout without writing any code. Shows the weight of different areas.Takes the focus away from colors and fonts, which clients may have very strong and unreasonable opinions about. Don't use real text in wireframes.

What does CSS look like?: Selector {property: value} External, embedded, or inline(don't do} Can specify absolute or relative sizing. Absolute sizing isn't accessible. Inheritance organized through document object model. A style assigned to the body will be inherited by everything below it.
